The Max Mayhem books are four urban fantasy novels I started writing in 2006. Set in Portland, OR, they are a prequel followed by a trilogy. The prequel, Lune (2009), is under revision. Bloodwood (2011) is available on Goodreads and Amazon. The Oddclock (2016) is available for publication. The final book, The Garden Dark, is unfinished.


Recklessly, Dr. Mayhem and the professor pass through the Moon Gate and discover Lune, land of perpetual night. Its people are trapped in a nightmare, in thrall to a dreaming Djinn. Dr. Mayhem and the professor must wake him, before they, too, give in to sleep.

A hunt for a missing girl leads Max and Lydia to an eerily empty town in the cold, northern mountains of Canada. When night falls, Bloodwood wakes, and it will take all of Max and Lydia's cunning to survive until sunrise.

Cursed skateboarder Max Mayhem and his friend, Lydia, befriend Henry Bishop, who has a dark and secret history. When they learn that the professor has been tracking Henry's father through time, their worlds threaten to tear apart. ~ 594 pages. Manuscript available on request.
